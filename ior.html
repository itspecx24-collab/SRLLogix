What is Importer of Record (IOR)?

SRL-LOGIX provides comprehensive Importer of Record services, acting as the legal entity responsible for ensuring imported goods comply with all local laws and regulations. Our IOR service eliminates the complexities of international trade compliance for businesses importing into South Africa and across the SADC region.

Key IOR Services Include:

  • Legal Compliance: Ensure all imports meet South African customs requirements
  • Documentation Management: Handle all import permits, licenses, and certificates
  • Tax & Duty Management: Calculate and pay applicable import duties and taxes
  • Customs Clearance: Complete customs formalities and inspections
  • Risk Assessment: Identify and mitigate import compliance risks
  • Record Keeping: Maintain all required import documentation

Ideal For:

Businesses without local presence
International e-commerce sellers
First-time importers
Companies seeking compliance assurance
