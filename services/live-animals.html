Why Choose Our Live Animal Transport?

SRL-LOGIX specializes in the safe and humane transportation of live animals. Our team is trained in IATA Live Animals Regulations (LAR) and ensures compliance with all international standards for animal welfare during transit.

Our Specialized Services Include:

  • IATA LAR Compliance: Full compliance with International Air Transport Association regulations
  • Temperature Control: Climate-controlled transport for sensitive species
  • Veterinary Supervision: Qualified veterinarians oversee animal handling
  • Customs Expertise: Specialized knowledge of animal import/export regulations
  • 24/7 Monitoring: Continuous monitoring during transit
  • Door-to-Door Service: Complete handling from origin to destination
