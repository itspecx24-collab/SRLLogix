Understanding IOR and EOR

Clear distinctions for your international trade needs

Importer of Record (IOR)

The IOR is responsible for ensuring imported goods comply with local laws and regulations. We handle:

  • Customs clearance procedures
  • Import duties and taxes payment
  • Product compliance certification
  • Import documentation preparation
  • Regulatory filings and permits

Exporter of Record (EOR)

The EOR ensures exported goods meet destination country requirements. We manage:

  • Export licensing and permits
  • Export documentation preparation
  • Trade compliance verification
  • Restricted goods handling
  • International trade regulations
